# Data decision register

This register links each data choice in the research extract to its code location, source basis, and paper-use boundary. It is meant to prevent undocumented sample or variable changes as the paper moves from data preparation to estimation.

## Sources still needing paper-level treatment

The register documents the data layer, and `docs/design_justification.md` documents the design crosswalk. The paper still needs a narrative literature review for the mechanism: institutional grant substitution, tuition discounting, and how Title IV caps may shape pricing or aid packaging. That review belongs in the paper's theory and identification sections rather than in this register.
